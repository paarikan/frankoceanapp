Co-Star

Sep 06, 2018

Co-Star is an astrology app created by a small team in Brooklyn with a focus on modern design and a clever viral mechanism that has led to quick growth.

In this document, I'll go through why, whether you personally enjoy it or not, astrology is a significant and growing market with a particularly desirable demographic and how the team behind Co-Star is well poised to become a dominant brand in the sector.

  1. Market
    1. More accepted
    2. New religion
    3. Larger witchcraft lifestyle
  2. Team
  3. Product
    1. Modern design
    2. Tech enabled
    3. Viral mechanism
    4. Demonstrated popularity
  4. Potential Business Models
    1. Subscription content
    2. Display ads
    3. E-commerce
  5. Risks
    1. Astrology loses popularity
    2. Another media property takes the market
  6. Potential Roadmap
    1. Daily two-person horoscopes
    2. New markets
